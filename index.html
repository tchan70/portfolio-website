EcoQuest in action

EcoQuest is a mobile application designed to promote environmental cleanup through engaging gamification. Built using React Native and Firebase, EcoQuest offers cross-platform capabilities and scalability. The app includes features like litter logging, quests, and leaderboards, encouraging users to participate in cleaning up their local environments.

With EcoQuest, users can log the litter they collect, embark on various environmental quests, and compete on leaderboards to see who can make the biggest positive impact. The app aims to make environmental activism fun and rewarding, leveraging technology to drive real-world change.
